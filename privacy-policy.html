Privacy Policy – STACKPOP

Last updated: June 5, 2026

STACKPOP ("we", "our", or "the game") is a free mobile game developed by Nythrand. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Player name – a display name you choose voluntarily, used only to show your score on the in-game leaderboard.
  • Game scores – your high score, submitted to our online leaderboard service (LootLocker).
  • Device & advertising data – collected automatically by Google AdMob to serve ads. This may include your device's advertising ID, approximate location (country/region), and device type. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Purchase data – if you purchase "Remove Ads", the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not store or see your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name and score on the leaderboard.
  • To serve advertisements via Google AdMob (unless you purchase Remove Ads).
  • To process in-app purchases via Google Play Billing.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

STACKPOP uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob – advertising
  • LootLocker – online leaderboard
  • Google Play Billing – in-app purchases

4. Children's Privacy

STACKPOP is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

5. Data Retention

Leaderboard entries (name + score) are stored on LootLocker's servers. You can request deletion by contacting us. All other game data (settings, progress) is stored locally on your device and deleted when you uninstall the app.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you by contacting us at the email below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
